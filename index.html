Lucky Philip Dube

Lucky Philip Dube (3 August 1964 - 18 October 2007) was a South African reggae musician and Rastafarian He is considered one of the best reggae artists of all time because of his contribution in reggae music. He recorded 22 albums in Zulu, English, and Afrikaans in a 25-year period and was South Africa's biggest-selling reggae artist. Dube was murdered in the Johannesburg suburb of Rosettenville on the evening of 18 October 2007.
